About Us

Welcome to Aggies Coffee House, where each cup of coffee is a celebration of flavor and a tribute to the rich coffee culture...

Our mission is to provide a unique coffee experience by selecting the finest beans, roasting them to perfection...

Beyond coffee, we're committed to community engagement. Aggies Coffee House isn't just a place to grab a coffee...

Our menu features not only a wide array of specialty coffees but also gourmet desserts and snacks sourced from local vendors...

Thank you for choosing Aggies Coffee House. Whether you're here to study, meet friends, or just enjoy a quiet moment...



Visit us to check out our:
  • Specialty Coffee
  • Gourmet Desserts
  • Music and Poetry Readings
  • Open Mic Night

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Aggies Coffee House, we believe that great coffee should not come at the expense of our planet...

We source our coffee beans from local and international farmers who practice sustainable farming...

Our coffee shop reduces waste by using biodegradable cups and packaging...

Energy conservation is another crucial aspect of our sustainability efforts...

Beyond environmental responsibility, we are committed to giving back to the community...

At Aggies Coffee House, sustainability is not just a policy; it's a promise...

Community Involvement and Events

At Aggies Coffee House, we believe in the power of community...

Each month, we organize events that range from local art exhibitions and live music nights...

We also collaborate with local schools, charities, and organizations on events...


Highlights of our annual events include:
  • The "Aggies Artisan Fair" – a showcase for local crafts and artworks.
  • "Coffee with a Cause" – where a portion of every coffee sold supports a local charity.
  • Monthly "Poetry and Pastry Night" – a cozy evening of poetry readings accompanied by our delicious homemade pastries.

We invite you to join us at our next event...
